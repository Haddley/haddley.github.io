What is prompt engineering

Prompt engineering is a technique used in artificial intelligence (AI) to optimize and fine-tune language models for particular tasks and desired outputs. Also known as prompt design, it refers to the process of carefully constructing prompts or inputs for AI models to enhance their performance on specific tasks.

What is context injection?

An effective way to provide context to your queries is by crafting more explicit and detailed prompts. This approach helps the model better understand your question and generate more accurate responses.
