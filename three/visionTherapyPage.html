About Vision Therapy

Vision therapy is a non-surgical, non-invasive treatment for vision problems that cannot be corrected with glasses or contact lenses. It involves a series of exercises and activities designed to improve visual processing and strengthen the eye muscles.

Our Services

Comprehensive vision exams

We provide comprehensive eye exams to assess your visual acuity and eye health. Our exams include a detailed analysis of your eye movements, eye teaming, and focusing abilities.

Customized vision therapy programs

We develop individualized vision therapy programs tailored to your specific needs and goals. Our programs typically involve weekly sessions and may last from a few weeks to several months.

Visual perceptual testing

We offer visual perceptual testing to assess your visual processing skills, including visual memory, visual spatial skills, and visual analysis.

Binocular vision assessment

We perform binocular vision assessments to evaluate how well your eyes work together. This includes tests of eye teaming, eye tracking, and depth perception.

Neuro-optometric rehabilitation

We provide neuro-optometric rehabilitation for patients with visual problems resulting from brain injury or neurological disorders.
