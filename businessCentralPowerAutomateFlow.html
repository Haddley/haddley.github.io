Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central (Part 20)

Power Automate Flow

By Microsoft, Public Domain

Power Automate Flow

Business Central includes a Microsoft Power Automate license.

Power Automate flows are triggered when Business Central records are created, modified, or deleted. Flows can also be run on a user-defined schedule or on demand.

I navigated to https://make.powerapps.com

I clicked on Flows and selected the + New flow | Instant cloud flow menu item

I provided a name for the flow

I selected the Dynamics 365 Business Central connector

I specified the environment (PRODUCTION)

I specified the company (Evaluation)

I specified the entity (customers)

I created an operation step. I selected the business central connector

I selected the Get records (V3) action

I specified the environment

I specified the company

I specified the API category (v2.0)

I selected am entity (customers)

I selected the Row ID (of the newly created customer)

I added an Outlook.com operation

I selected the Send an email (V2) action

I clicked the Sign In button

I specified the email destination

I specified the email Subject and message Body

I navigated to the Business Central Customers page and clicked the + New button

I selected the entity template

The entity was saved (even before a Name was entered)

I returned to the flow to review the run history

I reviewed my Outlook.com sent folder

I reviewed the sent email
